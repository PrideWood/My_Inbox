### Example Sentences

**Français**

1. Je viens de finir mes devoirs.
2. Je viens de finir mon exercice. Maintenant, je vais commencer un autre exercice.
3. Tu viens de faire la vaisselle. Maintenant, tu vas sortir avec tes amis.
4. Il vient de prendre un café. Maintenant, il va payer l'addition.
5. Nous venons d'aller au cinéma. Maintenant, nous allons parler du film.
6. Vous venez de vous réveiller. Maintenant, vous allez vous doucher.
7. Vous venez de descendre du bus. Maintenant, vous allez monter dans le train.
8. Ils viennent d'acheter un nouveau téléphone. Maintenant, ils vont l'utiliser.
9. Je viens de voir un ami à toi dans la rue.

**English**

1. I've just finished my homework.
2. I've just finished my exercise. Now, I am going to start a new exercise. 
3. You've just done the dishes. You are going to go out with your friends now.
4. He's just drunk a cup of coffee. He is going to pay the bill now.
5. We've just been to the cinema. We are going to talk about the movie now.
6. You've just woken up. You are going to take a shower now.
7. You've just gotten off the bus. You are going to hop on the train now.
8. They've just bought a new phone. They are going to use it now.
9. I've just seen a friend of yours on the street.
