注意比较对象应为同一性质的

|不同的情况|例句|
|---|---|
|比较主语|Country A has a bigger population than country B.|
|比较定语|The population of country A is bigger than that of country B.|
|compared with, incontrast to|Compared with country A, country B has a small population.|
|similar to, the same as, different from|The population of country A is different from that of country B.|
