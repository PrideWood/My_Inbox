|百分比表达方法举例|
|---|
|40% of students|
|the proportion of boys|
|boys account for 40% of students|
|the figure for boys is 40%|

|66%|two thirds of||
|55%|over half of||
|33%|one third of||
|25%|one quarter of|more than ten out of ten|
|10%|one tenth of|one in ten|
