## 书信

作文结构：
- 开头部分：写作目的+自我介绍（私人信件无需自我介绍）
	-  I am a staff member from your company/corporation. 
	- I am a freshman/sophomore/junior/senior undergraduate/graduate from the Department of Chinese Language and Literature. 
	- I am writing the letter **in purpose of** placing an order of some books.
	- I am writing to inform you that I wish to resign from my current post/position. 
- 中间部分
- 结尾部分：期待回信/表示感谢
	- My thanks for you to your generous help are beyond words. 
	- **Words fail me when** I want to express my sincere gratitude to you. 
	- I take this opportunity to show ==my heartfelt appreciation== to the kind assistance you **render** me.
	- I look forward to hearing from you soon.
	- I am looking forward to your favorable reply at your earliest convenience.
	- Your ==prompt attention== to my inquiry would be highly appreciated. 
- 结束语
	- 公务书信 Yours sincerely, Yours truly, Yours respectfully,
	- 私人书信 Yours sincerely, Yours lovingly, Yours,Yours cordially, Yours faithfully, Yours affectionately,
- 签名 Li Ming

## 告示

作文结构：
- 标题（必须写）

| 组成      | 示例                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 名词结构    | 1. 通知：Notice（通知）​，Announcement（通告）​，Notice of a Decision（决定通知）​，Notice of Dismissal（免职通知）<br>2. 启事：Removal（搬迁启事）​，Acknowledgements（鸣谢）​，Corrections（更正启事）​，Invitation for Bids（招标启事）<br>3. 其他：Note（便条）​，Preface（书刊序言）​，Pact（公约）​，Rules（守则/规则）​，Conditions for Car Rentals（租车注意事项）​，Christmas Clearance Sales（圣诞节清仓大甩卖） |
| 名词+过去分词 | Child Lost（寻子启事）​，Contributions Wanted（征稿启事）                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 现在分词+名词 | Looking for a Spouse（征偶启事）​，Exchanging Houses（换房启事）                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 介词结构    | For an Exchange of Jobs（换工作启事）                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |

- 日期：标题下一行的右侧，右端与右边线对齐。如“December 4, 2024”
- 正文（1~3段，一般为现在时或将来时）
	- 开头句型举例：
		- Your attention, please...
		- It has been decided that...
		- May I have your attention, please?
		- ... is/are requested to note that...
		- ... has/have the honor to announce...
		- ... takes/take the pleasure in announcing that...
		- ...has/have the pleasure to notice / announce (that)...
		- This is to note/notice/notify/announce that...
		- It is hereby noted/announced/proclaimed/made public that...
		- ...is/are authorized to invite/announce/declare that...
		- **Under the auspices of...**%% (idm 习语) under the auspices of sb/sth helped and supported by sb/sth; having sb/sth as a patron 在某人/某物的帮助或支持下; 有某人/某物赞助的 %%, a report/lecture/seminar on...will be given by...on...in...
		- Owing to / due to / because of / in order to..., we beg to inform...that...
		- Under the auspices of..., ...will organize/hold a...match/competition/contest between... and...on...in...
- 署名
