There are countless ways to **stay fit**. First and foremost, regular physical exercise is the most effective. Many people **incorporate** jogging, playing basketball, swimming, skating, climbing, or cycling **into** their routines because exercise always makes us feel stronger and more energetic. Additionally, **forming healthy habits** is essential. A balanced diet and ==sufficient sleep== are both crucial for maintaining good physical fitness.

As for myself, I find that exercising for an hour each day **leave**s me feeling healthier and more energized. I also maintain a balanced diet and make sure to go to bed before 11 p.m., as I believe that staying up too late is harmful to one's health.

In conclusion, these practices ensure a healthy body and contribute to a happy, fulfilling life.
