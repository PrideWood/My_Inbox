Human history is often viewed as the story of humanity’s migration from rural areas to cities, with urbanization seen as a symbol of human civilization. As depicted in the cartoon, while we enjoy the conveniences of city life, we also face numerous challenges **brought about** by urbanization, one of the most pressing being environmental pollution.

To start with, the rapid growth of urban populations **poses significant threats to** the natural environment. The rising number of ==city dwellers== has led to severe **ecological damage**, particularly through **the vast amounts of** household waste produced. Even when waste is buried or **incinerate**d, it can lead to soil or air pollution. Moreover, factories **discharge** hundreds of tons of wastewater into urban rivers daily, contaminating ==waterway==s **around the clock**. **Gone are the days when people could swim in clean rivers or fish along their banks.**

Therefore, it is crucial that we take action. First, we should urge authorities to **enforce strict laws** to **regulate urbanization**. Second, we must raise public awareness of the importance of sustainable development. Only through such efforts can we **mitigate the negative impacts** of urbanization and protect our environment.
